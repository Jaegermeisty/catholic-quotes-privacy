Overview

Catholic Quotes ("the App") is developed and operated by Mathias Jaeger-Pedersen ("we," "us," or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in connection with your use of the App.

Information Collection and Use

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

The App operates entirely offline on your device. All functionality, including:

  • Daily quote display
  • Liturgical calendar calculations
  • Quote shuffle system
  • Widget updates

...is performed locally on your device without any data being sent to external servers.

Data Storage

The App stores minimal data locally on your device using iOS UserDefaults:

  • Current position in quote rotation
  • Last update timestamp
  • Quote shuffle order

This data never leaves your device and is used solely to track which quote to display each day.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, advertising networks, or tracking tools.

Children's Privacy

The App is appropriate for users aged 4 and above. We do not knowingly collect any information from children or any other users.
